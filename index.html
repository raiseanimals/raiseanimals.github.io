Raise Animals: Build Your Dream Farm in This Adorable Roblox Simulator

What Is Raise Animals?

Raise Animals is a charming Roblox simulation game developed by Raise Animals Studio, where players dive into the joys of farming and animal husbandry. As a spin-off from the popular Grow a Garden, it shifts the focus from plants to pets, letting you collect, raise, and care for a diverse array of animals on your personal farm. Whether you're a newbie farmer or a seasoned tycoon, the game's relaxing yet engaging mechanics encourage creativity and progression. Start with a humble plot and a single starter animal, then expand into a bustling zoo that attracts tourists and yields profits. With over millions of visits and a thriving community as of October 2025, it's a must-play for animal lovers seeking offline growth perks and social sharing vibes.

Key Features at a Glance

  • Diverse Animals: From common farm staples like chickens and cows to exotic rarities like lions and elephants, each species brings unique passive abilities that boost your farm's efficiency.
  • Lasso Mini-Game: Venture into wild biomes for thrilling rodeo-style captures—ride your tamed mounts, leap onto targets, and build trust to claim new critters.
  • Stores and Trading: Chat with quirky NPCs to buy lassos, food, or animals; sell your grown stock for quick cash.
  • Index System: A handy catalog tracks your collection, reveals unlock requirements, and highlights abilities like speed boosts for faster captures.
  • Tourist Economy: Invite other players to your farm for passive income—flash rare mutations to draw bigger crowds.
  • Daily Quests and Mutations: Tackle quests for Rubies (premium currency) and watch weather events trigger random mutations that skyrocket sell values.

The game's whimsical art style, combined with satisfying progression loops, creates an addictive experience that's family-friendly and endlessly replayable.

How to Play Raise Animals Online: A Step-by-Step Guide

Raise Animals revolves around a simple yet deep cycle: Acquire animals, nurture them, and monetize your efforts. Here's how to get started and master the basics.

Getting Started: The Tutorial and Your First Farm

Upon launching the game, you'll be greeted by a brief tutorial that hands you your starter animal (often a bison or basic farm critter) and guides you through essentials. Head to the Food Store NPC first—interact with [E] to buy affordable feed. Equip it from your inventory, then click on your farm plot to place and feed your pet. Watch the hunger bar fill as it grows, producing resources like eggs or milk for selling. Pro Tip: Feed regularly to unlock passive abilities early, like increased tourist attraction.

Acquiring New Animals: Buying vs. Capturing

Direct Purchase: Visit the Animals Store for safe, instant buys. Common species cost little cash, but rarities demand Rubies or heavy grinding. Ideal for beginners building a stable core collection.

Wild Captures (Lasso Mini-Game): For the thrill, equip a lasso from the store and enter biomes via the in-game map (tap Play > Map > ? icon for animal previews). Ride your tamed animal as a mount, use [Space] to jump onto wild targets. Land successfully? A heart meter appears—stay on for 30-60 seconds to build trust without falling. Timing jumps is key; mistime it, and you'll tumble, resetting progress. Biomes like Savannah (for lions after 500-700 spawns) or forests offer themed spawns, with rarities boasting better sell values and abilities.

Higher-rarity animals (e.g., Rainbow variants at ~1% drop) shine in quests and tourist draws, so explore diverse maps unlocked with earnings.

Caring and Growing Your Herd

Once home, place animals on farm plots—they auto-grow, even offline. Feed to accelerate maturity, collect produce (e.g., wool from sheep), and monitor health via the Index. Mutations add excitement: Random weather (rain, storms) or events can transform animals into golden or rainbow forms, boosting value by 2-5x. No special items needed—just luck and patience.

Earning and Expanding

Sell produce or full-grown animals at stores for cash. Attract tourists by showcasing rares—each visit nets coins, scaling with your farm's appeal. Complete Daily Quests (e.g., "Capture 5 forest animals") for Rubies, used for premium unlocks like advanced lassos. Reinvest in map expansions to access new biomes and species, turning your plot into a profitable empire.

FAQs

How do animals get mutations in Raise Animals?

Mutations trigger randomly via weather (e.g., rain for shiny variants) or birth events—no items required. They enhance sell prices and abilities; rarer weather ups the odds

How can I get a lion in Raise Animals?

Head to the Savannah biome after 500-700 spawns. Use a strong lasso and mount for a ~1% shot at a Rainbow Lion. Check the map's ? icon for spawn tips.

Where can I find each animal in Raise Animals?

Open the in-game map (Play > Map > ?), which lists biomes and possible spawns. Forests for deer, oceans for exotic fish—explore to unlock all!

What is Raise Animals about?

It's a cozy simulator about capturing wild animals with lassos, raising them on your farm, feeding for produce/money, and selling for profits. Build a tourist hotspot while collecting rares

Can I play offline or get free boosts?
